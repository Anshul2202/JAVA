Naming Convention in java......

Class ----> First Alphabet should be capital

Interface ------> First Alphabet should be capital

Methods -----> Firts Alphabet should be small

Variables -----> First Alphabet should be small || Every variable should have a unique name || Only _ , $ , Numbers are allowed

Packages -----> Everything must be in small

Constants ------> Everything must be in capital

And for All of the Above :-
  Names should me meaningfull
  Should not very long
